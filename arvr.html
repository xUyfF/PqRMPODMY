Immersive Relaxation and Learning Spaces

Utilize AR/VR technology to create virtual relaxation spaces where students can escape for a few minutes of mindfulness or meditation. These spaces can also be tied to the learning content; for example, a history lesson could include a virtual tour of ancient Rome, or a biology lesson could involve a 3D exploration of the human body.
